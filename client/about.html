POLYSWEEPER - a two player competitive Minesweeper clone

RULES:

TIMED MODE: each player with their own board. Both player 1 and 2’s boards are displayed on either player’s screen, but each player can only interact with their own. The mine placements on both boards are identical, and both players must complete the board as fast as possible. Players can use the uncovered cells on their enemy’s board to their advantage to uncover cells.

TURN BASED MODE – both players play on one board, taking turns to uncover cells. Points are calculated opposite to the regular game – they are gained by uncovering a cell with a mine. A player’s turn continues as long as they only press cells with mines in them. If an empty cell is discovered, the next player has their turn. Whoever has the most points once the board is completed wins.
